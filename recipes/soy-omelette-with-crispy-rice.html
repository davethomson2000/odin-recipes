Soy Omelette With Crispy Rice

Image by pikisuperstar on Freepik

Description

The aim of this dish is to be as crispy as possible

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Cooked Sushi Rice
  • Cashew Nuts
  • Soy Sauce

Method

  1. Add cooked sushi rice to sesame oil in a wok until crispy
  2. Cook a basic Omelette with a little soy sauce added
  3. Combine, with cashew nuts for crunch.
